# Lord Voldemort (Tom Marvolo Riddle)

## Basic Information
- **True Name**: Tom Marvolo Riddle
- **Title**: Lord Voldemort, The Dark Lord, He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named
- **Age**: 68 (appears ageless after resurrection)
- **Blood Status**: Half-blood (secret shame)
- **Current Form**: Baby-like homunculus (until resurrection ritual)

## Stats (Post-Resurrection)
- **Magical Prowess (MP)**: 20 (+5 modifier)
- **Physical Prowess (PP)**: 12 (+1 modifier)
- **Mental Acuity (MA)**: 18 (+4 modifier)
- **Social Grace (SG)**: 16 (+3 modifier) - Charismatic when needed

## Derived Stats
- **Hit Points (HP)**: 100
- **Magical Stamina (MS)**: 60
- **Spell Save DC**: 18 (8 + 5 + 5)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +5
- **Legendary Resistance**: 3/day

## Pre-Resurrection Stats (Homunculus Form)
- **Hit Points (HP)**: 15
- **Magical Stamina (MS)**: 20
- **Completely dependent on Wormtail for survival**

## Abilities
- **Horcrux Immortality**: Cannot die while Horcruxes exist
- **Parseltongue**: Natural speaker
- **Legilimency**: Master-level mind reading
- **Flight**: Can fly without aid
- **Dark Magic Mastery**: All known dark spells

## Known Spells
### All spells up to Expert level
- **Avada Kedavra**: Signature killing curse
- **Crucio**: Torture curse
- **Imperio**: Mind control
- **Fiendfyre**: Uncontrolled dark fire
- **Soul Magic**: Horcrux creation and manipulation

## Background
- **Birth**: December 31, 1926, to Merope Gaunt and Tom Riddle Sr.
- **Childhood**: Wool's Orphanage, exhibited magical cruelty early
- **Hogwarts**: Slytherin, Head Boy, star student facade
- **Transformation**: Began experimenting with dark magic, immortality
- **Rise**: Gathered Death Eater followers, First Wizarding War
- **Fall**: October 31, 1981, failed to kill baby Harry Potter
- **Return**: Resurrection ritual in Little Hangleton graveyard

## Horcrux Timeline
1. **Tom Riddle's Diary** - First Horcrux, made after killing Myrtle
2. **Marvolo Gaunt's Ring** - Family heirloom
3. **Salazar Slytherin's Locket** - Stolen from Hepzibah Smith
4. **Helga Hufflepuff's Cup** - Also stolen from Smith
5. **Rowena Ravenclaw's Diadem** - Hidden in Room of Requirement
6. **Harry Potter** - Accidental Horcrux
7. **Nagini** - Living Horcrux

## Resurrection Requirements
- **Bone of the Father**: Unknowingly given (Tom Riddle Sr.'s bone)
- **Flesh of the Servant**: Willingly sacrificed (Wormtail's hand)
- **Blood of the Enemy**: Forcibly taken (Harry's blood)

## Personality
- **Megalomaniacal**: Believes in his superiority over all
- **Cruel**: Enjoys inflicting pain and fear
- **Intelligent**: Strategic genius, master manipulator
- **Paranoid**: Trusts no one completely
- **Narcissistic**: Cannot understand love or sacrifice
- **Patient**: Plans decades in advance

## Fears
- **Death**: Ultimate fear that drives all actions
- **Harry Potter**: The boy who lived, prophecy child
- **Dumbledore**: Only wizard he ever truly feared
- **Love**: Cannot understand or combat it

## Goals (Year 4)
1. Return to physical form
2. Eliminate Harry Potter
3. Rebuild Death Eater army
4. Conquer wizarding world
5. Achieve true immortality

## Death Eaters (Inner Circle)
- **Bellatrix Lestrange**: Most fanatical follower
- **Lucius Malfoy**: Financial and political influence
- **Severus Snape**: Double agent (unknown to Voldemort)
- **Barty Crouch Jr.**: Current most trusted servant
- **Peter Pettigrew**: Useful but despised

## Weaknesses
- **Horcrux Dependency**: Mortality tied to their existence
- **Brother Wand**: Priori Incantatem possible with Harry
- **Love Magic**: Cannot touch Harry due to mother's sacrifice
- **Arrogance**: Underestimates enemies
- **Inability to Love**: Cannot understand its power

## Combat Tactics
- **Overwhelming Force**: Uses most powerful spells immediately
- **Psychological Warfare**: Taunts and terrifies before killing
- **Minion Sacrifice**: Uses Death Eaters as shields
- **Strategic Retreat**: Will flee if outmatched
- **Torture**: Prefers to make examples of enemies

## Knowledge
- **Prophecy**: Knows partial version about Harry
- **Ministry Weaknesses**: Extensive intelligence network
- **Death Eater Locations**: Can summon all marked followers
- **Hogwarts Secrets**: Discovered many during student years
- **Ancient Magic**: Studied extensively but misses key elements
