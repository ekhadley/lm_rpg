# Barty Crouch Jr. / "Mad-Eye Moody"

## Basic Information
- **True Name**: Barty Crouch Jr.
- **Cover Identity**: Alastor "Mad-Eye" Moody
- **Age**: 35 (appears older as Moody)
- **Blood Status**: Pure-blood
- **Affiliation**: Death Eater (fanatical)

## Stats (True Form)
- **Magical Prowess (MP)**: 18 (+4 modifier)
- **Physical Prowess (PP)**: 14 (+2 modifier)
- **Mental Acuity (MA)**: 16 (+3 modifier)
- **Social Grace (SG)**: 12 (+1 modifier)

## Derived Stats
- **Hit Points (HP)**: 85
- **Magical Stamina (MS)**: 45
- **Spell Save DC**: 16 (8 + 4 + 4)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +4

## Cover Identity Stats (As Moody)
- **Magical Prowess (MP)**: 16 (+3 modifier) - Slightly reduced to match expected age
- **Physical Prowess (PP)**: 12 (+1 modifier) - Accounts for "injuries"
- **Mental Acuity (MA)**: 16 (+3 modifier)
- **Social Grace (SG)**: 8 (-1 modifier) - Moody's gruff nature

## Proficiencies
- **All MP Categories**: Master-level Dark Arts knowledge
- **Dueling** (PP): Expert combatant
- **Deception** (SG): Maintaining false identity
- **Investigation** (MA): Auror training (false memories)
- **Intimidation** (SG): Teaching through fear

## Known Spells
### All spell levels up to Expert
- **Unforgivable Curses**: All three, used without hesitation
- **Polyjuice Potion**: Master brewer
- **Memory Charms**: Expert level
- **Combat Spells**: Vast arsenal
- **Dark Curses**: Extensive knowledge

## Equipment
- **Wand**: 13 inches, walnut, dragon heartstring
- **Hip Flask**: Contains Polyjuice Potion (hourly doses)
- **Magical Eye**: Stolen from real Moody
- **Seven-Lock Trunk**: Contains real Moody in bottom compartment
- **Dark Detectors**: All functional but under his control

## Background
- **Family**: Son of Barty Crouch Sr., mother died in Azkaban taking his place
- **Escape**: Smuggled out by dying mother using Polyjuice
- **Captivity**: Kept under Imperius at home for years
- **Liberation**: Freed by Voldemort after Bertha Jorkins revealed truth
- **Mission**: Infiltrate Hogwarts, ensure Harry reaches graveyard

## Daily Routine
- **5:00 AM**: Check on real Moody in trunk
- **6:00 AM**: First Polyjuice dose of day
- **Every Hour**: Precisely timed Polyjuice doses
- **Night**: "Security rounds" - planning and reporting
- **Communication**: Modified Dark Mark for Voldemort contact

## Personality (Cover)
- **Paranoid**: "CONSTANT VIGILANCE!"
- **Harsh Teacher**: Believes in practical experience
- **Gruff**: Dismissive of weakness
- **Protective**: Of "worthy" students (those useful to plan)

## True Personality
- **Fanatical**: Completely devoted to Voldemort
- **Intelligent**: Genius-level strategic thinking
- **Ruthless**: Will kill without hesitation
- **Patient**: Years of planning and preparation
- **Sadistic**: Enjoys others' suffering

## Mission Objectives
1. Ensure Harry enters Triwizard Tournament
2. Prepare Harry to survive tasks and reach graveyard
3. Turn Cup into Portkey for final transport
4. Maintain cover identity at all costs
5. Eliminate threats to the plan

## Vulnerabilities
- **Polyjuice Dependency**: Must drink hourly or transformation fails
- **Trunk Discovery**: Real Moody's location
- **Behavioral Inconsistencies**: Doesn't match real Moody perfectly
- **Flask Timing**: Precise schedule observable
- **Magical Eye**: Doesn't belong to him

## Combat Tactics
- **No Mercy**: Uses Unforgivables immediately
- **Environmental**: Uses classroom traps and advantages
- **Escape Plans**: Multiple routes prepared
- **Kill Witnesses**: Won't leave evidence

## Relationships
- **Voldemort**: Absolute loyalty and worship
- **Real Moody**: Prisoner, source of memories and hair
- **Harry Potter**: Mission target, false protection
- **Dumbledore**: Dangerous enemy to deceive
- **Snape**: Suspicious of, avoids when possible
