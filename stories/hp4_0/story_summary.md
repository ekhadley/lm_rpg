# Harry Potter Year 4 - Story Summary

## Campaign Start: September 1st, 1994

### Background Context
- Harry Potter has just finished his third year at Hogwarts
- Recently attended the Quidditch World Cup with the Weasleys
- Dark Mark appeared at the World Cup, causing panic and fear throughout wizarding Britain
- Death Eaters were present at the World Cup, though they fled when the Dark Mark appeared
- The wizarding world is on edge - first sign of dark activity in 13 years
- Harry is about to begin his fourth year, unaware that this will be the year Voldemort returns

### Current Status
- Harry is at Platform 9¾, having just passed through the barrier
- The Hogwarts Express is ready to depart in 10 minutes
- Harry's trunk is loaded, he has Hedwig and his wand
- The atmosphere on the platform is tense due to recent events
- Harry needs to find a compartment and decide how to spend the journey

### Key Information Available
- Aurors are patrolling the platform (unusual security)
- Mrs. Weasley is being extra protective, hugging everyone twice
- Draco Malfoy is smirking while his father speaks in low tones
- Several families have decided not to return to Hogwarts this year
- Mad-Eye Moody's distinctive silhouette is visible near the train (though Harry doesn't know who he is yet)

### Opening Situation
Harry stands on Platform 9¾ with his loaded trunk, ready to board the Hogwarts Express for his fourth year. The usual excitement of returning to school is tempered by the fear and uncertainty following the Dark Mark incident at the World Cup. He must now decide how to approach this journey - seeking comfort with friends, investigating the unusual atmosphere, making new connections, or finding solitude to process recent events.
