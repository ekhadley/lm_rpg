# Harry Potter - Player Character (Year 4)

## Basic Information
- **Name**: Harry James Potter
- **Gender**: Male (he/him)
- **Blood Status**: Half-blood
- **Year**: 4
- **House**: Gryffindor

## Stats
- **Magical Prowess (MP)**: 14 (+2 modifier)
- **Physical Prowess (PP)**: 11 (+0 modifier)  
- **Mental Acuity (MA)**: 10 (+0 modifier)
- **Social Grace (SG)**: 8 (-1 modifier)

## Derived Stats
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +2 (Year 4)
- **Magical Stamina (MS)**: 20/20 (10 + 8 + 2)
- **Hit Points (HP)**: 18/18 (10 + 8 + 0)
- **Spell Save DC**: 12 (8 + 2 + 2)

## House Abilities
- **Heroic Surge** (Year 1): Once per long rest, gain temporary MS equal to Year + Proficiency Bonus (6 temporary MS). This bonus MS disappears after 10 minutes or 5 turns. If you end up with less than 0 MS, you faint for an hour.
- **Swish and Flick** (Year 4): Once per combat, you may cast two offensive spells in a single action.

## Proficiencies
- **Flying** (PP-based) - Natural Quidditch talent
- **Dueling** (PP-based) - Defensive instincts from multiple encounters
- **Charms** (MP-based) - Gryffindor house proficiency
- **Perception** (MA-based) - Gained Year 2 from constant danger
- **Standard Spells** (MP-based) - Gained Year 3 from extensive practice
- **Nonverbal Casting** (MP-based) - Gained Year 4 from advanced training

## Known Spells

### Basic Spells (DC 10, 1 MS)
- **Lumos** - Creates light from wand tip
- **Reparo** - Mends broken objects
- **Flipendo** - Knockback jinx (1d4 force damage + 10ft push)

### Intermediate Spells (DC 15, 3 MS)
- **Expelliarmus** - Disarming charm (target drops item + 1d4 force damage)
- **Confundo** - Confundus charm (target confused for 2 rounds)
- **Protego** - Shield charm (negates one attack/spell, can be cast as reaction)

### Advanced Spells (DC 20, 5 MS)
- **Petrificus Totalus** - Full body-bind curse (target immobilized 2 rounds)
- **Expecto Patronum** - Patronus charm (3d8 radiant vs Dementors, requires happy memory)
- **Bombarda Maxima** - Blasting curse (3d8 force damage in 30ft radius)

## Background & Family
- **Parents**: James and Lily Potter (deceased October 31, 1981)
- **Godfather**: Sirius Black (recently discovered, in hiding)
- **Raised by**: The Dursleys - Vernon, Petunia, and Dudley (abusive Muggle relatives)
- **Original Home**: Godric's Hollow (destroyed)
- **Current Home**: Hogwarts (considers it true home)
- **Inheritance**: Considerable gold in Gringotts vault 687, parents' invisibility cloak

## Equipment
- **Wand**: Holly wood, phoenix feather core (from Fawkes), 11 inches, nice and supple - brother wand to Voldemort's
- **Pet**: Hedwig, female snowy owl
- **Broom**: Firebolt (anonymous gift from Sirius, Year 3)
- **Special Items**: 
  - Invisibility Cloak (Deathly Hallow, though unknown)
  - Marauder's Map (shows all of Hogwarts)
  - Two-way mirror piece (from Sirius)
  - Sneakoscope (from Ron)
  - Photo album of parents (from Hagrid)

## Personality & Traits
- **Core Traits**: Brave to the point of recklessness, fiercely loyal, strong moral compass, impulsive
- **Fears**: Dementors (they make him hear his parents' deaths), losing those he loves
- **Strengths**: Natural leader, excellent under pressure, powerful defensive magic
- **Weaknesses**: "Saving people thing," struggles with Occlumency, fame discomfort
- **Habits**: Touches scar when stressed, terrible at lying, nightmares about Voldemort

## Key Relationships
- **Ron Weasley**: Best friend since Year 1, like a brother
- **Hermione Granger**: Best friend since Year 1, voice of reason
- **Sirius Black**: Godfather, father figure, secret correspondent
- **Remus Lupin**: Former professor, father's friend, mentor
- **Albus Dumbledore**: Headmaster, protector, keeps secrets
- **Severus Snape**: Potions master, mutual hatred
- **Draco Malfoy**: School rival, son of Death Eater
- **Cho Chang**: Crush, Ravenclaw Seeker
- **Cedric Diggory**: Respected competitor, fellow Hogwarts champion

## Current Status (Start of Year 4)
- **Location**: The Burrow, then Hogwarts
- **MS**: 20/20
- **HP**: 18/18
- **Recent Events**: Witnessed Voldemort's servant escape, learned truth about parents' betrayal
- **Special Knowledge**: 
  - Can speak Parseltongue
  - Connection to Voldemort causes scar pain/visions
  - Knows about Horcruxes (not yet)
  - Parents were Head Boy/Girl
  - Father was an Animagus (Prongs)

## Combat Capabilities
- **Preferred Spells**: Expelliarmus (signature), Expecto Patronum (corporeal stag)
- **Combat Style**: Defensive, disarming focused, uses environment
- **Special Advantages**: 
  - Exceptional reflexes (Seeker training)
  - Brother wand to Voldemort's (Priori Incantatem possible)
  - Experience against Dark creatures
  - Heroic Surge for desperate moments
