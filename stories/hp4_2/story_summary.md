# Story Summary - Harry Potter Year 4

## Session 1: Journey to Hogwarts
**Date**: September 1st, 1994
**Time**: Morning

### Pre-Journey Context
- The Quidditch World Cup recently ended with a Death Eater riot and the Dark Mark appearing in the sky
- Harry spent the remainder of summer at the Burrow with the Weasleys
- The Daily Prophet has been spinning various narratives about the World Cup incident

### Current Status
- Harry is at King's Cross Station with the Weasley family
- About to board the Hogwarts Express for his fourth year
- Current tracks: Press Reputation 0, Authority Suspicion 0, Tournament Standing 0, Dark Mark Tension 1 (elevated after World Cup)
