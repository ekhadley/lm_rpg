# Kathy Nightingale - Player Character

## Basic Information
- **Name**: Kathy Nightingale
- **Gender**: Female (she/her)
- **Blood Status**: Half-blood
- **Year**: 1
- **House**: Hufflepuff

## Stats
- **Magical Prowess (MP)**: 12 (+1 modifier)
- **Physical Prowess (PP)**: 8 (-1 modifier)  
- **Mental Acuity (MA)**: 12 (+1 modifier)
- **Social Grace (SG)**: 8 (-1 modifier)

## Derived Stats
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +0 (Year 1)
- **Magical Stamina (MS)**: 11/13 (10 + 2 + 1, used 1 for Lumos on train, 1 for second night, 1 for retry in corridor)
- **Hit Points (HP)**: 11/11 (10 + 2 - 1)
- **Spell Save DC**: 9 (8 + 1 + 0)

## House Abilities
- **True Friend** (Year 1): When using the Help action to aid an ally's check, they gain a +2 bonus to their roll in addition to Advantage.

## Proficiencies
- **Healing Spells** (MP-based)
- **Investigation** (MA-based)
- **Herbology** (MA-based) - Gained from Hufflepuff House

## Known Spells
- **Episkey** (Basic Healing, DC 10, 1 MS) - Heals minor wounds for 1d6+1 HP
- **Flipendo** (Basic Jinx, DC 10, 1 MS) - 1d4 force damage + pushes 10 ft
- **Lumos** (Basic Charm, DC 10, 1 MS) - Creates light from wand tip

## Background & Family
- **Mother**: Slytherin alumna, works at the Ministry of Magic. Warm and protective but ambitious for her daughter's success. Encourages Kathy to excel at everything.
- **Father**: Muggle fantasy writer of middling success. Easygoing, funny, always trying to make Kathy laugh. Has seen much of the magical world since marriage.
- **Home**: Near Ottery St. Catchpole
- **Discovery of Magic**: Learned about magic one year ago when she first showed signs. Made her mother prove magic was real through demonstration.

## Equipment
- **Wand**: Apple wood, phoenix feather core (from Fawkes), 11.5 inches - healing magic affinity
- **Owl**: Archimedes, long-eared owl with golden eyes
- **Books**: Complete first-year set plus "Magical Drafts and Potions"
- **Supplies**: Standard potions kit, telescope, quills, parchment, etc.

## Personality & Traits
- **Core Traits**: Bookish, curious, excited about magic, healing-focused
- **Fears**: Letting her mother down, not making friends at Hogwarts
- **Interests**: Reading excessively (fantasy and sci-fi like her father)
- **Social Situation**: Found close friends in Emmett, Lyra, and Marcus
- **Appearance**: Short and scrawny with jet black hair kept in a plait
- **Current State**: Thrilled to be sorted into Hufflepuff, feeling like she belongs

## Relationships
- **Emmett Thornwood** (Hufflepuff, First Year): Close friend from Diagon Alley
- **Lyra Ashworth** (Ravenclaw, First Year): Close friend, brilliant researcher
- **Marcus Vale** (Slytherin, First Year): Close friend, understands loss
- **Sorting Hat**: Warned about "shadows in the stones," trusted her completely

## Current Status
- **Location**: Hufflepuff basement corridor, night
